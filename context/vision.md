# Vision & Goals

_Last updated: 2026-05-27_

---

## North star

**Take the pain out of home maintenance.**

Home maintenance is universally dreaded. JustFix exists to make it feel like the least painful thing you did this week — not because it's free or instant, but because it's effortless. The customer books, the fixer arrives, the job is done. That's it.

---

## Strategic position

JustFix is the **easiest** home maintenance solution, not the cheapest.

We compete on:
- **Convenience** — fewer steps, faster booking, less coordination required
- **Certainty** — customers know what to expect (cost, timing, who's coming)
- **Trust** — vetted fixers, real reviews, a platform that stands behind the work

We do not compete on price. Customers who are primarily cost-sensitive are not our customers.

---

## Current strategic priorities

### 1. Deepen the AI estimation advantage

AI-generated cost estimates have proven a 2x conversion lift over the traditional hourly rate path. This is a core differentiator and should be extended across surfaces (web, ChatGPT app, Claude app) and refined to be more accurate and personalised over time.

### 2. Strengthen the fixer experience

Fixer reliability is a key risk to customer trust. The migration from SMS/web app to WhatsApp Flows aims to create a more reliable, app-like touchpoint that reduces no-shows and admin friction for fixers.

### 3. Close the native/web app gap

The native app is significantly out of step with the web app. As an occasional-use product, the native app may not be the right priority — but the inconsistency creates trust issues when customers do encounter it.

### 4. Develop the Claude app

The Claude app is not yet in discovery. It should follow the same AI estimation model proven on the web app and ChatGPT app, with discovery to identify what Claude-specific capabilities (e.g., multi-turn conversation, reasoning) can add unique value.

## What good looks like

A customer discovers a leak under their sink on a Tuesday morning. By Tuesday lunchtime they've booked a plumber, received a cost estimate they're comfortable with, and haven't had to make a single phone call. The plumber shows up Wednesday, fixes it, and the customer's only interaction with JustFix afterwards is a review prompt. That's the product we're building.
