# Ideal Customer Profile

_Last updated: 2026-05-27_

---

## Primary customer: the time-poor homeowner

Our customer is someone who owns or rents a home and has a maintenance problem they need fixed — but who doesn't have the time, energy, or confidence to navigate the traditional trades market themselves.

**Core characteristic: convenience over cost.** JustFix is not the cheapest option and we do not compete on price. Our customers are willing to pay a premium to avoid the friction of finding, vetting, and managing a tradesperson themselves.

### Jobs to be done

- Get a problem fixed without it consuming my weekend or multiple phone calls
- Know roughly what it's going to cost before committing
- Trust that the person arriving at my home is competent and safe
- Not have to think about it again after booking

### Frustrations with the status quo

- Getting three quotes takes days and requires being home for each one
- Hourly rate quotes give no sense of total cost — anxiety-inducing
- Hard to tell if a tradesperson is actually good
- Unpredictability: will they show up? Will the job go over?

### What they value from JustFix

- Speed: book and forget
- Cost certainty: AI-generated estimates remove price anxiety (validated: 2x conversion vs hourly rate — see `decisions.md`)
- Trust: vetted fixers with reviews
- Simplicity: as few steps as possible between "I have a problem" and "it's booked"

---

## Secondary customer: the fixer

Fixers are vetted tradespeople who receive and complete bookings through JustFix. They are a distinct audience with different needs.

**Core characteristic:** wants reliable job flow without admin overhead.

### Jobs to be done

- See and accept relevant jobs quickly
- Have all the information needed to do the job well (location, scope, access notes)
- Log work and get paid without paperwork friction
- Trust that the platform has their back if a customer is difficult

### Current touchpoints

- SMS invite with booking URL → simple web app for accept/check-in/feedback
- Migrating to WhatsApp Flows for a more app-like experience

---

## Who we are NOT building for

- Customers who primarily shop on price — they will churn when they find cheaper
- DIYers who want advice — we are a booking platform, not a how-to service
- Commercial property managers (not current focus)
- Fixers who want full business management software — we serve the JustFix job slice only

---

## Notes for the agent

When writing any customer-facing copy or designing any customer journey, orient decisions around: **does this make it faster, clearer, and less effortful for the time-poor homeowner?**

When writing for fixers, orient around: **does this reduce admin friction and give them what they need to do the job?**

These are distinct audiences. Do not conflate them.
